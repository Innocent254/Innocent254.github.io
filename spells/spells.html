The Three Grand Fairy Spells

  • These are the three great spells invented by the masters of Fairy Tail
  • They are Fairy Law, Fairy Glitter and Fairy Sphere.
  • There are certain conditions required to be met in order to be used.

    • Fairy Glitter

    Fairy Glitter

  • This one of the 3 grand fairy spells of fairy tail.
  • It requires an enormous amount of concentration for one to weild it
  • It is usually embeded on the hand of the caster.
  • This is seen on Kana Albarona twice; First when at Tenrou Island, then at the Grand magic Games

    Fairy Sphere

  • It is spell that is able to defend the casters from that which seems to harm them
  • It requires more than one participant
  • It uses the power of bonds and emotions for it to work.

    Fairy Law

  • This is a spell that vanquishes evil using sacred light
  • It destroys all whom the caster sees as their enemy
  • In return the life of the caster is deducted every time one uses it
  • This is why it is considered one of the dark spells
